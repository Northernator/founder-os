# ICP -- smoke-test

## Summary

The ideal customer profile for 'smoke-test' is defined by three main personas. Firstly, product development teams at tech startups and SMBs who need efficient tools to manage workflows with a focus on speed and AI integration. These teams value systems that enhance coordination and product velocity. Secondly, larger enterprise organizations looking for customizable, all-in-one solutions enabling them to automate and streamline complex workflows, integrating seamlessly with existing tools. Lastly, individual tech-savvy freelancers or small business owners requiring feature-rich, user-friendly tools for managing diverse aspects of project management in an integrated platform.

## Persona 1: Startup Product Development Manager

_id: `startup-product-development-manager`_

These are managers or team leads of tech startups or SMBs, where teams are small but dynamic, regularly juggling tight deadlines and rapid product iterations. Their role involves overseeing product development workflows and ensuring high team productivity with fewer resources.

**Pain points**

- Struggle to maintain high velocity and focus amidst team growth.
- Need efficient integration between various AI and team workflows.
- Lack of tools facilitating seamless coordination among team members.

**Primary goal**

Help me integrate AI-driven workflows so that my team can maintain high productivity with minimal friction.

## Persona 2: Enterprise Workflow Specialist

_id: `enterprise-workflow-specialist`_

A professional within large enterprises responsible for optimizing team workflows and system integrations. They deal with complex organizational processes and require highly customizable solutions that support a wide range of integrations.

**Pain points**

- Difficulty in customizing workflows to meet specific team needs.
- Challenges in integrating with existing enterprise systems and tools.
- Managing complex and multi-layered team structures without loss of efficiency.

**Primary goal**

Help me automate complex workflows so that enterprise operations are streamlined and efficient.

## Persona 3: Freelancer or Small Business Owner

_id: `freelancer-or-small-business-owner`_

Independent professionals or owners of small businesses who manage all aspects of their projects. They use digital tools extensively to organize tasks and workflows, needing solutions that are comprehensive yet simple to handle.

**Pain points**

- Overwhelmed by managing multiple tools and integrations for various tasks.
- Need for an all-in-one platform to consolidate workspace and communication.
- Troubles managing project timelines efficiently without complex software.

**Primary goal**

Help me unify my project management tasks so that I can spend more time on delivering quality work.
